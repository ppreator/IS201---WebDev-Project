Why I Love Golf

Golf has been one of my favorite ways to spend time outdoors, whether I'm playing with my dad or just working on my own game at the local course. It's a sport that takes patience, focus, and a lot of practice — but the moment you hit a perfect shot, it's worth every frustrating swing before it.
